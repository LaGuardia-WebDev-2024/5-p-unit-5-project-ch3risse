Fun facts!

"Hollywood attempted a film adaptation of Into the Woods at least three times before. In the early 1990s Columbia Pictures announced it was working on an adaptation with a cast that was to have featured Cher as the Witch, Robin Williams as the Baker, Goldie Hawn as the Baker's Wife, Steve Martin as the Wolf and Danny DeVito as the Giant. Nothing came of that beyond a reading," writes Playbill.

Stephen Sondheim has received eight Tony® Awards, more than any other composer in history. He has won seven times: Best Music and Best Lyrics for “Company” (1971); and BestScore for “Follies” (1972), “A Little Night Music” (1973), “Sweeney Todd” (1979), “Into the Woods” (1988) and “Passion” (1994).
